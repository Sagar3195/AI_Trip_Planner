# Welcome to Rome : 
Rome, the capital of Italy, is a city rich in history, art, and architecture. This travel guide is tailored to provide an engaging and informative experience for travelers interested in sightseeing and good food. 
Rome is home to numerous iconic landmarks, historical spots, and cultural attractions. Some must-visit places include:
* The Colosseum: An ancient amphitheater and one of Rome's most recognizable symbols.
* The Vatican City: An independent city-state within Rome, home to the Pope and numerous iconic landmarks such as St. Peter's Basilica and the Sistine Chapel.
* The Roman Forum: A historic site featuring ancient ruins, temples, and basilicas.
* The Pantheon: A magnificently preserved ancient temple with an impressive dome.
The city offers a wide range of accommodation options, from luxury hotels to budget-friendly hostels. Some popular areas to stay in Rome include the city center, Trastevere, and Monti.
